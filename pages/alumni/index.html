Alumni

Home / Alumni

NIT’s alumni — climatologists, thermodynamicists, manufacturers, and above all, professional thermometer users — have helped to make the temperature tools we know and use today. From researching mercury to engineering everything else of a thermometer, many of the products that define the world of temperature we live in, have sprung from the brains and fists of NIT’s hottest talents.

NIT’s nearly 1.3 million alumni represent one of the most talented, innovative, and thermally accurate communities on the planet. Many remain deeply involved in the life of the Institute. They volunteer, serve on boards, head up regional alumni clubs, and generously polish NIT's many thermometers, faculty, and staff. As individuals, their thermal contributions are extraordinary. As a community of thermometer lovers, their impact is incredible.
