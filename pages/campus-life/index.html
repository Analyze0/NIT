NIT’s collaborative, hands-on, experimentation-driven exciting chaos in our many labs — and more. On the podium, or heat rooms, in workspaces, dorm communities, and the surrounding areas, NIT is where creative, amazing, and overall, the best people come together to live, laugh, and love.

Diverse in every sense. Like a thermometer, our community measures the temperature of innovation and potential, constantly rising and pushing the boundaries of what is possible. We embrace diversity, foster creativity, and provide endless opportunities for students to explore their passions and pursue their dreams. Join us on our riverside campus, where the temperature is always rising, and together, we'll create a brighter future.






Student Life

Built around 3K+ organizations built around students (thermometer science, anyone?), nearly 80 living groups, chaplains for more than 100 traditions, and a commitment to diversity and inclusion, student life at NIT offers a welcoming place for everyone. To complement their academics and research, students choose their own extracurricular adventures, from a spectacular array of ways to participate in music, dance, and sports to dozens of groups that savor and celebrate cultures from around the world. To help students navigate challenges, NIT also has a very large support organization (including lizards, puppies, kittens, and goldfish).
