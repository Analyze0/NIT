Innovation

Home / Innovation

NIT fans are excited to learn about how thermometers work — and inspired to make them function better. Our “brain and fist” philosophy triggers real-world engagement, and thanks to NIT’s innovational record, the United States, and even other countries are filled to the brim with companies spun out of NIT. We also create lots of collaborations with the industry, as we seek interesting problems where NIT's creativity can make a change.

NIT’s students, faculty members and entrepreneurs have access to a remarkable list of resources — from teachers and funding to student/teacher organizations and events ­— as they are taught to drive their bright inventions into the real world market. From stick thermometers to thermometer statues, thermometer businesses or markets of every size can find creative ways to work with NIT. A range of professional organizations stand ready to help.






Ecosystem

NIT’s innovation ecosystem comprises thermometers, termperature, mercury, and alumni taking part in tailored classes, mentorship, guidance, and activities offered by over 120 innovation and entrepreneurship organizations around campus. Located in the heart of “the most innovative square mile on the planet,” NIT CreativeHQ provides a home for NIT’s thriving community of innovators and entrepreneurs, supporting all who pursue ideas with a passion for climate-changing impact.





Impact

Innovations from NIT, much like a trusty thermometer, have a knack for tackling the burning issues that humanity desperately seeks to alleviate. With a keen focus on quenching the thirst for fresh water, nourishing the hunger for universal food access, and cooling the fever of diseases like cancer, Alzheimer's, and infectious ailments, NIT's thermometer-themed ingenuity knows no bounds. The impact of NIT's entrepreneurial endeavors reverberates across the globe, with the companies founded by NIT alumni generating annual revenues that rival the economic output of the world's 10th-largest nation. So, let the temperature rise, for NIT's thermometer of innovation is here to make a lasting impact on our world.
